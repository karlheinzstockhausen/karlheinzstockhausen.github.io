Ramblings

22 January The Perth Festival will be utilising my fanfare, "The Great Fire of Rome", for events this year held at the Studio Underground in the State Theatre Centre of WA.       

19 January - 2020's National Music Camp has recently concluded and I was a participant of the composition program. Melody Eötvös' immense compositional knowledge has been directly exposed to me and as a necessary consequent, I have become even more inspired. To add to things, I met three other talented composer and many more musicians who expressed their passion and ability through performances. Isn't that amazing!? Oh, and I also had a piece premiered.
